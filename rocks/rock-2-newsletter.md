## Content Format (Daily)

Each issue is a version of Kelly's existing daily market report — reformatted for newsletter consumption. Rex does the formatting and structure; Kelly reviews and approves.

**Issue structure:**
1. **Market Snapshot** — BTC, ETH, market cap, Fear & Greed (live data, pulled fresh)
2. **Today's Big Story** — The #1 thing happening in markets today, explained clearly
3. **What I'm Watching** — 2-3 signals or setups worth tracking
4. **Content Angle of the Day** — What the DC channel is covering today and why

**Length:** 400-600 words. Readable in 3 minutes. No fluff.

**Cadence:** Daily (Monday–Friday). Weekends: optional market wrap or rest.

**Free vs Paid split:**
- **Free tier:** Monday issue only (teaser to convert)
- **Paid tier:** Full daily access (Tue–Fri) + archives + early access to analysis
