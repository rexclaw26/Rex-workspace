# Synthesis Agent Prompt (use when both research files are complete)

You are a top-tier business strategist, brand consultant, and product developer. Your job is to synthesize competitive research on 10 digital product ideas and select the top 3 to build into full businesses.

Read both of these files completely before doing anything else:
- /Users/rex/.openclaw/workspace/product-research/research-ideas-1-5.md
- /Users/rex/.openclaw/workspace/product-research/research-ideas-6-10.md

## Context
- Brand: Discover Crypto (part of Hit Network)
- Audience: 1.4M YouTube subscribers, 1M+ X/Twitter followers, 500K Instagram followers, 3.7K Discord members
- Founder: Kelly — deeply embedded in crypto, DeFi, AI, media business building
- Rex (AI) can build: full websites, sales funnels, landing pages, email sequences, brand identity systems, dashboards, bots, APIs, content pipelines — with minimal Kelly input after initial direction
- Revenue target: fastest path to meaningful MRR, with the ability to sustain and grow beyond 6 months

## Selection Criteria (rank all 10 on these three dimensions):
1. **4-Week Profit Probability** — Can this generate real revenue within 4 weeks of launch? Factors: simplicity of product, existing audience fit, low build complexity, clear buying trigger.
2. **6-Month Sustained Success** — Does this have staying power? Factors: recurring revenue potential, evergreen demand, defensible moat, community component.
3. **Brand Strength** — Is there a compelling, ownable brand identity here? Factors: memorable name potential, visual identity potential, emotional resonance with target audience, differentiation from generic crypto noise.

## Your Task

### Part 1 — Challenge Every Idea
For each of the 10 ideas, write a brutal honest "Devil's Advocate" paragraph. What's the strongest argument AGAINST building this? What would kill it?

### Part 2 — Score & Rank All 10
Score each idea on all 3 dimensions (1–10 each). Show your math. Produce a ranked list of all 10 with total scores.

### Part 3 — Select Top 3
The top 3 do NOT need to sweep all three dimensions. But together as a portfolio they should cover all three. Pick the 3 with the best combination of scores AND strategic fit with the Discover Crypto brand and audience.

For each of the top 3, produce:

#### Brand Concept
- **Product name** (2-3 options, with reasoning)
- **Tagline** (punchy, 6 words or less)
- **Brand personality** (3 adjectives)
- **Color palette** (primary, secondary, accent — with hex codes)
- **Logo direction** (describe the visual concept in one paragraph)
- **Tone of voice** (how does this brand talk?)
- **URL suggestion** (short, available, memorable)

#### Business Model
- **Price point and structure** (one-time / subscription / tiered)
- **Upsell stack** (what's the upsell path?)
- **Distribution strategy** (how does it reach the Discover Crypto audience first?)

#### 15-Slide Deck Outline
Produce the exact text/data for all 15 slides. Use this structure:
1. Hero — name, tagline, one-line description
2. The Problem
3. The Solution
4. Market Opportunity (use real data from research, cite sources)
5. Competitive Landscape (real competitors from research)
6. Target Customer Profile
7. Product Details
8. Pricing & Offer Stack
9. Revenue Projections (4-week / 3-month / 6-month / 12-month) — be conservative, show assumptions
10. Brand Identity
11. Go-to-Market Strategy
12. Content & Marketing Plan
13. Sales Funnel Architecture
14. 90-Day Execution Plan
15. Why This Wins
