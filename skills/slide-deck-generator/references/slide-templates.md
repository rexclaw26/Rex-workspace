# Slide Deck Structure Templates

## 1. Standard Presentation
Best for: internal updates, educational content, team meetings

1. Title slide (topic + presenter + date)
2. Agenda
3. Context / Background
4. Main Point 1 + evidence
5. Main Point 2 + evidence
6. Main Point 3 + evidence
7. Key Takeaways
8. Next Steps / Ask
9. Q&A
10. Appendix (supporting data)

---

## 2. Pitch Deck (Investor / Sponsor)
Best for: funding rounds, sponsor proposals, partnership pitches

1. Title + one-liner (what is Hit Network in one sentence)
2. Problem (the gap in the market)
3. Solution (how Hit Network solves it)
4. Market Opportunity (TAM/SAM/SOM with sources)
5. Product / Content (what we actually make)
6. Traction (metrics, growth, social proof)
7. Business Model (how we make money)
8. Competitive Landscape (positioning matrix)
9. Team
10. Financial Projections (3-year, 3 scenarios, all PROJECTED)
11. The Ask (what we need, what they get)
12. Appendix: detailed financials, methodology, FAQs

---

## 3. Sponsor Proposal
Best for: brand partnership outreach

1. Title + brand
2. Who We Are (Discover Crypto / Blockchain Basement at a glance)
3. Our Audience (demographics, size, engagement — sourced)
4. The Opportunity (why this partner fits)
5. Sponsorship Packages (tiers with deliverables)
6. Past Results / Case Studies (if available)
7. Pricing & Terms
8. Next Steps

---

## 4. Weekly / Monthly Business Review
Best for: internal EOS scorecard, ops review

1. Title + date
2. Scorecard (KPIs vs. targets — RAG status)
3. 90-Day Rocks progress
4. Wins this period
5. Issues (IDS format)
6. Action items + owners + due dates
