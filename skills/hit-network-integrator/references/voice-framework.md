# Humanization & Voice Framework

Full protocol lives in the `humanization-voice` skill. Key rules summarized here for reference.

## Universal Rules
1. Use contractions (min 5 per 500 words in emails/reports; min 3 in presentations)
2. Zero em dashes connecting thoughts
3. No robotic transitions (banned: Furthermore, Additionally, Moreover, In conclusion)
4. Mix sentence lengths: short punch → longer explanation → medium bridge → short punch
5. Start sentences with And/But occasionally (at least 1 per major section)
6. Use fragments for emphasis sparingly: "Done." / "Not even close."

## Output Humanization Levels
- Email: MAXIMUM
- Report/Analysis: HIGH
- Presentation: MODERATE
- Social Media/X: MAXIMUM
- Script/Show Notes: VERY HIGH
- Internal Docs: MODERATE
