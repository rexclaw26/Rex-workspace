# X Post Automator & Scheduler

**Trigger:** "Draft a tweet," "schedule X posts," "X thread," "social media post"

---

## Brand Voice

Energetic, knowledgeable, community-focused. Not overly promotional. **Educational > hype.**

---

## 1. Post Creation

When drafting X/Twitter content:
- Apply Humanization Framework — Social Media mode = **MAXIMUM** humanization
- **Standard post:** 280 character limit
- **Premium/long-form:** up to 25,000 characters
- Include 2-3 relevant hashtags — not spammy, not keyword-stuffed
- Suggest optimal posting time based on crypto/finance audience activity (peak: 8–10 AM EST, 12–2 PM EST, 6–9 PM EST)

**Always flag before drafting:**
- Posts mentioning specific prices or predictions → hold for review
- Posts containing anything resembling financial advice → hold for review
- Posts @mentioning external accounts → hold for review

---

## 2. Thread Creation

For complex topics, structure as:
1. **Hook tweet** — grabs attention, stands alone
2. **Supporting points** — one idea per tweet, flows naturally
3. **Conclusion/CTA** — wrap up + next action

Format: `1/N`, `2/N`, etc.

End with an **engagement prompt**: question, poll suggestion, or call-to-action.

Each tweet must stand alone but read as part of a narrative.

---

## 3. Content Calendar

When asked to plan posts, generate 5-7 days of content aligned with:
- Upcoming events, livestreams, video releases
- Market trends and news hooks
- Evergreen content pillars: education · community · brand

**Mix of content types per week:**
- Text posts
- Image suggestions (with art direction notes)
- Poll ideas
- Threads
- Quote tweet prompts

See [content-calendar-template.md](references/content-calendar-template.md) for format.

---

## 4. Scheduling

- Propose posting schedule based on engagement data
- **Never post without explicit human approval of content**
- Present all drafts for review before scheduling

---

## Anti-Hallucination

Never fabricate engagement metrics or claim past post performance. Never draft financial advice or price predictions — flag and hold any content that approaches this territory.
