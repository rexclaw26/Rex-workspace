# Discover Crypto — X Post Voice & Format Guidelines

*Source: Kelly via email (kelly@bitlabacademy.com) — 2026-03-06*

---

## Core Format Rules

### Posts Under 75 Characters
- Keep as-is — short, punchy, no formatting changes needed.

### Posts Over 75 Characters
- **Break into statements with line breaks** — write like effective sales copy for easy digestion.
- Each idea or beat gets its own line.
- No walls of text. Breathing room between thoughts.

### BREAKING NEWS Format
- Start with uppercase **`BREAKING:`**
- Follow with an **ALL-CAPS, super succinct, catchy headline**
- Headline must be **no more than 2 lines**
- Then body copy (can be normal case + sales copy formatting below)

**Example:**
```
BREAKING: BLACKROCK SLAMS THE GATES ON ITS $10T FLAGSHIP FUND

Liquidity crunch hits the giant.
Massive redemption requests - withdrawal limits triggered.

MARKET MELTDOWN COMING?
```

## Voice Characteristics (Extracted from Example Posts)

### Tone
- Urgent, energetic, provocative — always pushing the reader to engage
- Genuine opinions — Kelly shares his actual POV, not just news
- Rallies the community ("Disco Fam", "you are not bullish enough")
- Mix of alarm and optimism — acknowledges pain while holding the bull thesis
- Occasionally personal / human (dog posts, mindset posts, humor)

### Sentence Style
- Short punchy statements > long sentences
- Ellipses used for dramatic effect: `...`
- Questions drive engagement: "Do you think it's gonna get worse?"
- Caps for emphasis on key terms: `BITCOIN`, `BREAKING`, `MARKET MELTDOWN`
- Rhetorical calls-to-action: "RT!", "Share if you agree!", "You are NOT bullish enough!"
- First-person conviction: "I've been buying heavy under $70K"

### Structure Patterns (for longer posts)
```
[HOOK — capitalized or bold claim]

[CONTEXT — 1-2 lines unpacking it]

[DATA POINT or FACT — specific number/stat]
[DATA POINT or FACT]

[CLOSING QUESTION or CALL TO ACTION]
```
