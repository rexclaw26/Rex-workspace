# Strategic Output Templates

## Executive Memo Format

```
MEMO
To: Kelly
From: Forge / Rex
Date: [date]
Re: [Topic — one clear subject line]
Confidence: High / Medium / Low — [1-sentence rationale]

─────────────────────────────────────────

RECOMMENDATION
[Lead with the answer. One paragraph, 3-5 sentences max.]

KEY FINDINGS
• [Finding 1] [Source: ...]
• [Finding 2] [Source: ...]
• [Finding 3] [Source: ...]

OPTIONS CONSIDERED
1. [Option A] — [why considered, why not recommended]
2. [Option B] — [why considered, why not recommended]
3. [Recommended option] — [why this one wins]

RISKS & FAILURE CONDITIONS
"What would have to be true for this to fail?"
• [Risk 1] — Mitigation: [approach]
• [Risk 2] — Mitigation: [approach]

NEXT STEPS
□ [Action 1] — Owner: [person] — Due: [date]
□ [Action 2] — Owner: [person] — Due: [date]
```

## Weekly Trends Brief Format

```
TRENDS BRIEF — Week of [date]
Source: Hacker News, ProductHunt, ArXiv, a16z, X/Twitter, TikTok

─────────────────────────────────────────

TREND 1: [Trend name]
What's happening: [2-3 sentences] [Source: ...]
Relevance to Hit Network: [High / Medium / Low]
Recommended action: [specific, actionable]

TREND 2–5: [repeat format]

─────────────────────────────────────────

ON THE RADAR (not breaking yet, worth watching)
• [Signal] — [why it matters]
```
