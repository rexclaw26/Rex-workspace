# Post-Production Checklist

Run before marking any video ready for publish.

## Hook & Pacing
- [ ] First 3 seconds grab attention — no slow intros or dead air
- [ ] Pacing reviewed — dead air trimmed, energy maintained throughout
- [ ] Jump cuts smooth and intentional

## On-Screen Text & Graphics
- [ ] Lower thirds on all speakers (name + title/handle)
- [ ] Brand bumper at intro and outro
- [ ] All charts and data overlays accurate and sourced
- [ ] Logo overlays correct (company/person referenced)
- [ ] Graphic quality matches brand standards

## Audio
- [ ] Levels normalized (target: -14 LUFS for YouTube, -16 LUFS for podcasts)
- [ ] Background noise reduced
- [ ] Music bed mixed under dialogue (not competing)
- [ ] No clipping or distortion

## Captions & Subtitles
- [ ] Captions present and timed accurately
- [ ] No auto-caption errors (especially names, ticker symbols, crypto terms)
- [ ] Font and style match brand guidelines

## End Screen & CTAs
- [ ] End screen added (last 20 seconds)
- [ ] Subscribe CTA visible
- [ ] Next video or playlist linked
- [ ] Social handles displayed

## Platform-Specific Checks
- [ ] Aspect ratio correct for target platform (16:9 YouTube / 9:16 Shorts·TikTok·Reels)
- [ ] Thumbnail created and reviewed (see thumbnail-generator skill)
- [ ] Title and description ready for upload
- [ ] Tags/keywords prepared

## Final Gate
- [ ] Watched full edit at 1x speed
- [ ] No brand, factual, or compliance issues
- [ ] Human approval obtained before publishing
