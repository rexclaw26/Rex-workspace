# Thumbnail Generator / Mood Board

**Trigger:** "Thumbnail," "mood board," "video thumbnail," "visual concepts"

---

## 1. Thumbnail Concepts

For each video, generate **3 thumbnail concepts** with detailed visual descriptions.

**Each concept includes:**

| Element | Specification |
|---------|-------------|
| Layout | Face placement, text overlay position, background treatment |
| Emotion | Facial expression that conveys the video's energy (shock, excitement, concern, curiosity) |
| Text | 3-5 words max — large, bold, readable at 1-inch mobile size |
| Color scheme | High contrast, brand-aligned, platform-optimized |

**Always deliver minimum 2 variations** for A/B testing.

See [thumbnail-patterns.md](references/thumbnail-patterns.md) for proven layout patterns and crypto channel references.

---

## 2. Thumbnail Best Practices

- **Face visible + clear emotion** — shock, excitement, concern perform highest in crypto
- **Text readable at mobile thumbnail size** (test mentally at 1-inch square)
- **High contrast colors** — avoid dark-on-dark or busy/cluttered backgrounds
- **Include relevant crypto logos/symbols** when topic-specific (BTC, ETH, protocol logos)
- **A/B test every video** — at least 2 variations, track CTR difference

---

## 3. Mood Board Creation

For video series, brand campaigns, or website redesigns, deliver:

- **Color palette** — hex codes with usage notes (primary, accent, background, text)
- **Typography suggestions** — headline font, body font, sizing hierarchy
- **Imagery style** — photography direction, illustration style, icon language
- **Layout patterns** — grid, spacing, composition principles
- **Reference examples** — what's working in the crypto/finance media space

Brand alignment: Hit Network · Discover Crypto · Blockchain Basement

See [moodboard-format.md](references/moodboard-format.md) for structure.

---

## 4. Performance Tracking

- Suggest tracking CTR by thumbnail style to identify what converts
- Compare: face vs. no face, text-heavy vs. minimal, color palettes, emotion types
- **Quarterly recommendation format:**
  > "Your best-performing thumbnail style is [X] because [Y]. Recommend applying this pattern to [specific video types]."

---

## Anti-Hallucination

Never fabricate CTR data or performance metrics. Use real channel analytics when available. If data isn't available, make recommendations based on documented best practices and flag as `[Best practice — verify with your analytics]`.
