## Free vs. Paid Tier Split

The key to driving upgrades: free tier teases the value, paid tier delivers the full depth.

| Content Type | Free Tier | Paid Tier |
|---|---|---|
| Breaking news summary | ✅ Top 3 stories | ✅ + full analysis + what it means for your portfolio |
| Market data | ✅ Price snapshot | ✅ + on-chain signals + institutional flow data |
| Weekly analysis | ✅ Key takeaway only | ✅ Full thesis + trade positioning context |
| Deep dives | ❌ Teaser only | ✅ Full report |
| Regulatory tracker | ✅ Headline | ✅ Full breakdown + implications |
| Exclusive research | ❌ | ✅ Original data, charts, proprietary analysis |
| Q&A / AMA access | ❌ | ✅ Monthly live Q&A with Kelly / contributors |
| Early access | ❌ | ✅ Content published to paid list 24-48 hrs early |

## Recommended Cadence

| Frequency | Format | Audience |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Daily (Mon-Fri) | 5-min brief: top story + 1 insight | Free tier |
| Weekly (Sunday) | Deep dive: 1 major topic fully analyzed | Paid tier |
| Monthly | State of the market + on-chain report | Paid tier |
| Breaking news | Instant alert (when 8+ virality + relevance score) | Both (paid gets full analysis faster) |

## Content That Converts Free → Paid

These content types have the highest free-to-paid conversion rates in crypto newsletters:

1. **Exclusive on-chain data interpretation** — "Here's what Glassnode is showing that most people aren't talking about"
2. **Actionable trade context** — Not financial advice, but "here's the framework sophisticated investors are using to think about this"
3. **Regulatory early warning** — "This bill just passed committee — here's what it means 6 months from now"
4. **Deep protocol analysis** — Full breakdown of how a DeFi protocol actually works, risks, and opportunities
5. **Macro-to-crypto translation** — "The Fed just said X. Here's exactly how that historically flows into crypto markets"
6. **Early access to research** — Whatever you're publishing to the public tomorrow, paid subscribers get today

---

## Upgrade Conversion Triggers

Build these into every free newsletter:

- **The cliff-hanger tease:** "We ran the on-chain numbers on this — paid subscribers get the full breakdown today"
- **The value gap:** Show the free analysis, then "The full thesis (including our positioning framework) is in today's paid edition"
- **Social proof:** "742 paid subscribers got this analysis 24 hours before it went public"
- **Urgency for time-sensitive content:** "The regulatory window closes Friday — full breakdown for paid subscribers now"

## Path to $10K MRR by June 2026

At $10/mo per subscriber:
- **Target: 1,000 paid subscribers** = $10K MRR
- At $20/mo: **500 paid subscribers** = $10K MRR

**With 2.9M+ total reach across platforms:**
- 1,000 paid subscribers = 0.03% conversion rate — highly achievable
- Focus: convert the most engaged 1% of YouTube subscribers and Discord members first

**Conversion funnel:**
1. YouTube → "subscribe to our free newsletter for daily crypto briefings"
2. Free newsletter (daily) → delivers value, builds habit
3. Free → paid conversion: paywall the weekly deep dive, tease it in free edition
4. Discord → exclusive newsletter-subscriber channel (community adds to paid value)

**Pricing recommendation:** Start at $15/mo or $120/yr (2-month discount). Don't underprice — signals quality. Don't overprice — signals friction.

## Newsletter + Content Calendar Integration

Every monthly content calendar should pre-assign:
- Which weekly deep dive topics go to the paid newsletter
- Which free newsletter topics align with that week's YouTube video (reinforce, don't repeat)
- Which breaking news topics warrant an instant alert (use news-aggregation 8+ score as trigger)
- One "exclusive" per month for paid subscribers that's NOT available on any other platform

The newsletter is not a recap of what's already on YouTube. It's complementary: YouTube shows, newsletter analyzes.
