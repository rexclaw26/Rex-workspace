# Content Repurposing Workflow — Hit Network 2026

## The Core Principle: Extract More, Create Less

Companies using systematic repurposing generate 30-50 distinct content pieces from a single long-form asset. With AI tools, a lean team can run a full repurposing engine in ~2 hours per piece vs. 12-15 hours manually.

Every YouTube video is already a gold mine. The problem isn't content volume — it's extraction discipline.

---

## The Repurposing Waterfall

Start with one pillar piece. Cascade it across formats:

```
PILLAR PIECE (Long-form YouTube OR deep-dive article)
    ↓
3-5 YouTube Shorts (30-60 sec clips)
    ↓
X/Twitter thread (7-10 tweets)
    ↓
Newsletter section (free OR paid tier)
    ↓
SEO article (if YouTube was the source)
    ↓
Quote graphic / infographic
    ↓
Podcast audiogram (if audio is clean)
```

## Format-to-Channel Mapping

| Source Asset | Repurposed Format | Channel |
|---|---|---|
| Full YouTube video (10-30 min) | 5-10 short clips (30-60 sec) | YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels |
| YouTube video audio | Podcast episode | Spotify, Apple Podcasts |
| Video key points | X/Twitter thread (7-10 tweets) | X |
| Video analysis/insights | Long-form SEO article | Blog / Discover Crypto website |
| Blog article key takeaways | Newsletter section | Email list |
| Article stats/charts | Quote graphics / infographics | Instagram, LinkedIn |
| Best 60-sec moment | YouTube Short with CTA | YouTube Shorts |
| Newsletter section | 3-tweet thread teaser | X |

---

## Prioritization Tiers

Not everything gets full repurposing treatment. Score each asset:

**Tier 1 — Full stack repurposing:**
- High engagement (views/comments/reactions) + search potential + evergreen topic
- Gets: article + newsletter + X thread + Shorts + audiogram

**Tier 2 — Rapid social only:**
- High engagement + trending/time-sensitive topic
- Gets: 3-5 Shorts + 1 X thread (no long-form — it'll be stale by the time it's done)

**Tier 3 — Archive refresh:**
- Old top performer + opportunity to update with current data
- Gets: republish article with updated stats + 1 new Short
