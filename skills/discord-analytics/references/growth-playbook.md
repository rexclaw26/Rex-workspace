# Discord Community Growth Playbook

Data-driven recommendations based on observed patterns.

---

## Retention Triggers

| Signal | Threshold | Recommended Action |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Retention rate dropping | <70% weekly | Audit onboarding flow, add welcome automation |
| New member message rate low | <30% message in first 7 days | Improve #welcome channel prompt, add onboarding bot |
| Top contributors leaving | Any high-contributor departs | Direct outreach, role recognition review |

---

## Engagement Boosters (by data signal)

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Message volume flat 2+ weeks | Launch an event (AMA, giveaway, prediction contest) |
| Specific channel dying | Pin a conversation starter, promote in announcements |
| Sentiment trending negative | Surface pain points to team, transparent community update |
| Hot topic emerging | Create a dedicated thread or channel, ride the wave |

---

## Channel Health Rules

- **Archive** if: no messages in 14+ days AND topic is covered elsewhere
- **Revitalize** if: channel has history/value but went quiet — pin a starter, promote
- **Split** if: a channel is handling 2+ distinct topics (reduces noise)
- **Merge** if: two channels have overlapping topics and both are low-traffic

---

## Growth Levers

1. **Video-to-Discord pipeline** — every video drop should drive Discord CTA
2. **X-to-Discord funnel** — X threads end with Discord invite for deeper discussion
3. **Role gamification** — reward active members with exclusive roles and perks
4. **AMAs with hosts** — high-engagement, high-retention events
5. **Exclusive alpha** — Discord members get content/info before anywhere else
