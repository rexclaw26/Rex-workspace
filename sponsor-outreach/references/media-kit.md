## Sponsor Philosophy

We only partner with brands that genuinely serve our audience. We don't:
- Promote unvetted altcoin or token projects
- Run misleading claims or unverifiable ROI promises
- Partner with brands that conflict with our editorial independence

We do work with:
- Bitcoin & crypto financial services (IRAs, exchanges, custody)
- Hardware wallets & security products
- Crypto-adjacent fintech and financial tools
- Education platforms
- AI and technology brands relevant to our audience
