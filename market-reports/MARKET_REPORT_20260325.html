The tokenization hearing and the CLARITY Act timeline are inseparable. The CLARITY Act determines whether a given tokenized asset is a digital security (SEC) or a digital commodity (CFTC). Today's hearing is Congress formally asking the right questions about legal architecture. The CLARITY Act is the legislation that answers them.

DeFi Trader Loses $50 Million in Failed Swap; MEV Bot Captures $34.5 Million in Single Block
